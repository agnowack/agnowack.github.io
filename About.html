A little about me

I was extremely excited about the opportunity and immediately agreed to spend as much of my time there as he would allow. I started out with small tasks
like reimaging company computers and eventually I began going to client sites. It was a thrill to help set everything up and learn just how much is involved in
providing technology related services. For instance once of the clients that I helped service is a Montessori school, I was amazed at the amount of IT infastructure
that is involved in running a school.It was a great way to get introduced to the field of IT.
One of the things that I believe is most important in life, is to always keep moving forward. I am working toward getting an AAS degree in Cybersecurity
My plan is dual enrollment using homeschool and Wake Tech (Go Eagles!). I hope to earn useful certificates for IT such as the CompTIAF+.
One of my current focuses is setting up a proper website to increase traffic flow. An influx of diverse traffic leading to diverse projects will allow me more experience
and enhance my skills, making me more valuable to the marketplace. In the future one of the services I will offer will be designing and setting up websites tailored to any need
one may have.
